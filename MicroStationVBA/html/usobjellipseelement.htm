EllipseElement Object

                                                     

A kind of Element that corresponds to a MicroStation arc element.

Remarks

You cannot use the New keyword to create an EllipseElement object. Rather, use one of the global functions like CreateEllipseElement1 and its variants or ConstructCirclesTangentToThreeElements.

MicroStation does not have a distinct circle type. A circle is an ellipse with PrimaryRadius and SecondaryRadius equal.
