SecondPartials3d Type

        

Represents the second partial derivatives of a u,v-parametric three-dimensional surface at a point on the surface.

The SecondPartials3d type has these members:

Name Description
Duu A Point3d that represents the second u-partial derivative vector at a point on the surface.
Dvv A Point3d that represents the second v-partial derivative vector at a point on the surface.
Duv A Point3d that represents the mixed uv-partial derivative vector at a point on the surface.
Dvu A Point3d that represents the mixed vu-partial derivative vector at a point on the surface.

Remarks

For points in a ModelReference's geometry, the values are measured in the model's coordinate system, unless stated otherwise.

For sufficiently smooth surfaces like B-spline surfaces, the vector Duv is called the twist vector, and equals Dvu.

The method BsplineSurface.EvaluatePointDerivatives2 returns the second partial derivatives of a B-spline surface at a point specified by its parametric coordinates.
