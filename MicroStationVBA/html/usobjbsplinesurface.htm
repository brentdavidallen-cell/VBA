BsplineSurface Object

                                                     

Corresponds to the MDL B-spline surface structure, MSBsplineSurface.

Remarks

As per MDL analogue, most of the manipulation of a BsplineSurfaceElement in VBA is performed on a BsplineSurface object, and only at the last step is an element created (cf. CreateBsplineSurfaceElement1) or updated (cf. UpdateFromBsplineSurface) from this object.

The properties of a BsplineSurface object may be set directly, or they may be populated by various BsplineSurface methods. When a BsplineSurface property is changed, dependent properties are adjusted as necessary to maintain a valid object state. For example, toggling BsplineSurface.UClosed installs uniform UKnots (if UPolesCount and UOrder are valid).

To get a "blank" BsplineSurface object, use the New keyword.
