FirstPartials3d Type

        

Represents the first partial derivatives of a u,v-parametric three-dimensional surface at a point on the surface.

The FirstPartials3d type has these members:

Name Description
Du A Point3d that represents the partial derivative vector with respect to u at a point on the surface.
Dv A Point3d that represents the partial derivative vector with respect to v at a point on the surface.

Remarks

For points in a ModelReference's geometry, the values are measured in the model's coordinate system, unless stated otherwise.

For a given point, the vector Du is the tangent of the u-isoparametric curve of the surface through that point; the vector Dv is the tangent of the v-isoparametric curve of the surface through that point.

The cross product (cf. Point3dCrossProduct) of the vectors Du and Dv is in the direction of the surface normal at the point.

The methods BsplineSurface.EvaluatePointDerivatives1 and BsplineSurface.EvaluatePointDerivatives2 return a FirstPartials3d containing the first partial derivatives of the B-spline surface at a point specified by parametric coordinates.

The method BsplineSurface.EvaluatePointCurvature returns a FirstPartials3d structure containing unit vectors in the directions of the minimum and maximum curvatures at a non-umbilic point specified by parametric coordinates. Like the first partial derivatives, these vectors lie in the tangent plane of the surface at that point.
