Transform3dFromRowValues Method

                          

Syntax

Transform3d = object.Transform3dFromRowValues (X00, X01, X02, Tx, X10, X11, X12, Ty, X20, X21, X22, Tz)

The Transform3dFromRowValues method syntax has these parts:

Part Description
object A valid object.
X00 A Double expression. The (0,0) entry of the matrix (row, column)
X01 A Double expression. The (0,1) entry
X02 A Double expression. The (0,2) entry
Tx A Double expression. The x-coordinate of the translation part
X10 A Double expression. The (1,0) entry
X11 A Double expression. The (1,1) entry
X12 A Double expression. The (1,2) entry
Ty A Double expression. The y-coordinate of the translation part
X20 A Double expression. The (2,0) entry
X21 A Double expression. The (2,1) entry
X22 A Double expression. The (2,2) entry
Tz A Double expression. The z-coordinate of the translation part
