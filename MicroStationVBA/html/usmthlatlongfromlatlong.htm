LatLongFromLatLong Method

                          

Converts the longitude, latitude, and elevation from the Datum of this GeographicCoordinateSystem to the Datum of another GeographicCoordinateSystem

Syntax

GeoPoint3D = object.LatLongFromLatLong (Point, OtherSystem)

The LatLongFromLatLong method syntax has these parts:

Part Description
object A valid object.
Point A GeoPoint3D expression. The longitude, latitude, and elevation for which the Datum shift is to be performed.
OtherSystem A GeographicCoordinateSystem expression. The GeographicCoordinateSystem that designates the datum to which the longitude, latitude, and elevation are to be shifted.

Remarks

In most cases other than NAD27 to or from NAD83, the elevation values are unchanged.
