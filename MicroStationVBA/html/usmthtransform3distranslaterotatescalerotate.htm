Transform3dIsTranslateRotateScaleRotate Method

                          

Factor a transformation as a product of a translation, two pure rotations, and a (sandwiched) scale, i.e. 

                 Transform = Translation * Rotation1 * Scale * Rotation2

where

  • Translation is a pure translation
  • Rotation1 and Rotation2 are pure rotation about the origin
  • Scale is a principle axis scale, applied in a rotated coordinate system

Once again:

  • Rotation1 and Rotation2 are pure rotation matrices. Each is orthogonal. Each column has magnitude 1. Each has determinant 1. Each defines a right handed coordinate system.
  • Scale contains x, y, and z scale factors. If converted to a matrix, the scale factors appear on the diagonal and the off-diagonals are zero. The largest absolute value scale factor is the x scale, and the smallest absolute value scale factor is the z scale. The z scale (but no others) may be negative, which means that the transformation performs reflection.

    • If the composite matrix is applied to a or point X, the equational form is

                       transform * X = Translation * Rotation1 * Scale * Rotation2 * X

    If you read this from right to left to interpret it as a sequenc of actions on X, X is rotated around the global origin by Rotation2, scaled along the rotated coordinate axes, rotated again, and finally translated.

    The rotations and scales are internally arranged so that the largest scale factors are first in the scale point.

    If you have perservered this far, you may recognize that the product Rotation1*Scale*Rotation2 is a singular value decomposition. (With small caveat that in some definitions of the singular values, all the scales are non-negative and the reflection effects make one of the rotations into a reflection.)

    Syntax

    Boolean = object.Transform3dIsTranslateRotateScaleRotate (Transform, Translation, Rotation1, ScaleFactors, Rotation2)

    The Transform3dIsTranslateRotateScaleRotate method syntax has these parts:

    Part Description
    object A valid object.
    Transform A Transform3d expression. IN transform being analyzed.
    Translation A Point3d expression. OUT The translation, as a Point3d
    Rotation1 A Matrix3d expression. OUT One of the rotation parts, as a Matrix3d
    ScaleFactors A Point3d expression. OUT scale factors, as xyz parts of a DPoint3d.
    Rotation2 A Matrix3d expression. OUT The other rotation part, as a Matrix3d
