MsdTextJustification Enum

        

Defines constants that represent the various ways that text can be justified (aligned) within its region.

The MsdTextJustification enum has these members:

Name Description
msdTextJustificationLeftTop 0 (&H0)
msdTextJustificationLeftCenter 1 (&H1)
msdTextJustificationLeftBottom 2 (&H2)
msdTextJustificationCenterTop 6 (&H6)
msdTextJustificationCenterCenter 7 (&H7)
msdTextJustificationCenterBottom 8 (&H8)
msdTextJustificationRightTop 12 (&HC)
msdTextJustificationRightCenter 13 (&HD)
msdTextJustificationRightBottom 14 (&HE)
